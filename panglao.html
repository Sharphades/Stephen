Panglao Island

Panglao Island is known for its pristine beaches, luxury resorts, and vibrant marine life, making it one of Bohol's top destinations.

Things to Do:

  • Relax on Alona Beach and Dumaluan Beach.
  • Go diving or snorkeling in world-class dive spots.
  • Explore Hinagdanan Cave or enjoy island-hopping tours.

Entrance Fee: Free (fees apply for specific activities).

Location: Panglao, Bohol
