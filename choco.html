Chocolate Hills

The Chocolate Hills, located in Carmen, Bohol, are a geological formation of over 1,000 hills that resemble chocolate drops during the dry season. They are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a top attraction in the Philippines.

Things to Do:

  • Visit the viewing deck for panoramic views.
  • Explore the nearby ATV trails.
  • Take photos during sunrise or sunset.

Entrance Fee: PHP 50

Location: Carmen, Bohol
