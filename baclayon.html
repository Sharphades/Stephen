Baclayon Church

Baclayon Church, also known as the Church of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception, is one of the oldest churches in the Philippines, showcasing Baroque architecture and historical artifacts.

Things to Do:

  • Explore the church's museum.
  • Admire the intricate carvings and relics.
  • Attend a mass or take a guided tour.

Entrance Fee: PHP 50 (museum fee)

Location: Baclayon, Bohol
