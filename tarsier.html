Tarsier Sanctuary

The Tarsier Sanctuary in Corella, Bohol, is home to the tiny and endangered Philippine tarsiers, known for their large, expressive eyes.

Things to Do:

  • Observe tarsiers in their natural habitat.
  • Learn about tarsier conservation efforts.
  • Take a guided tour of the sanctuary.

Entrance Fee: PHP 60

Location: Corella, Bohol
