Loboc River

The Loboc River is a serene waterway in Bohol where visitors can enjoy a unique floating restaurant experience or take a boat cruise surrounded by lush greenery.

Things to Do:

  • Enjoy a buffet on the floating restaurant.
  • Take a sunset cruise along the river.
  • Experience live cultural performances during the cruise.

Entrance Fee: PHP 450 (buffet included)

Location: Loboc, Bohol
