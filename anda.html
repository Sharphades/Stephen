Anda Beach

Anda Beach is a hidden gem in Bohol, offering powdery white sand, crystal-clear waters, and a peaceful ambiance away from the crowds.

Things to Do:

  • Swim and relax in the calm waters.
  • Enjoy a picnic by the beach.
  • Explore nearby caves and springs.

Entrance Fee: Free

Location: Anda, Bohol
