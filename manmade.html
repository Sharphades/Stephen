Man-Made Forest

The Man-Made Forest is a 2-kilometer stretch of mahogany trees, offering a cool and serene atmosphere perfect for nature lovers.

Things to Do:

  • Take scenic photos amidst the towering trees.
  • Go for a peaceful walk or drive through the forest.
  • Spot local wildlife in the area.

Entrance Fee: Free

Location: Bilar, Bohol
